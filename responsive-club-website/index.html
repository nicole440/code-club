Lancaster Code Cooperative

Who we are

We are individuals of all ages, abilities, and from all walks of life, who come together over a shared passion for coding. We exchange ideas, help one another, and learn from each other.
We are Lancaster Code Cooperative.

Join us remotely or in person for opportunitites for pair-programming, local networking, and collaboration.
All are welcome.

We meet on the second and third Sunday of each month from 2:00 p.m. to 4:00 p.m., as well as weeknights at varying times. See the calendar below for precise meeting information.
We'd love to see you there.
